Data Science

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that focuses on extracting meaningful insights from vast amounts of data. It combines aspects of statistics, computer science, and domain-specific knowledge to interpret complex datasets. Students will learn data cleaning, analysis, and modeling techniques using programming tools like Python, R, and SQL.

More Info.

Data Analytics

Data Analytics is focused on using data-driven techniques to solve business problems. This course teaches students how to analyze, interpret, and communicate data to inform business decisions. Emphasis is placed on using software tools to uncover trends and patterns that can drive efficiency and profitability.

Details

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence explores the creation of intelligent systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. Students will learn about machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, and robotics. The course also covers ethical considerations in AI development.

Industrial Vist

The industrial visit to Fopple Drone Tech Private Limited was organized by PB Siddhartha College of Arts and Science for the BSc students specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (5th semester). The objective was to provide students with practical exposure to the innovative application of drone technology in the agricultural sector.

Fopple Drone Tech Private Limited (FDTPL) is a registered startup operating as a Private Limited Indian Non-Government Company incorporated in India on 27 June 2017 (Six years and six months 7 days old. Its registered office is in Krishna, Andhra Pradesh, India.
